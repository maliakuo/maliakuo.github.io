What is a bird?, Spring 2022

My final project was centered around explorations in art technologies. I chose to focus on how many ways I could represent a bird in different mediums - from pencil and paper to sculpture (both traditional and digital) to an interactive game.

Unity

What if *we* were the bird? Using Unity, I modeled a basic bird model and created a custom controller to gameify the bird. The idea is that you are a bird, exploring this forest and flying or hopping around the ground.


AR for Local New Haven Mural, Spring 2022

Unity

The aim of this project was to craft an augmented reality experience in Unity that interacted with a local New Haven mural. The mural depicts the partnernship of New Haven bus drivers and essential workers in keeping the city safe during Covid. To this end, we first met with the artist to discuss their motivations and intention regarding the piece. Following this conversation, my group settled on our design and executed it, which involved an animated bus driving through the mural as well as flowers that appear as you interact with and move closer to the mural.
