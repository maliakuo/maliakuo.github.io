For my senior project, designed a real-time collaborative, audio-visual collaging website using web-sockets.

The Installation, Nov 2024

tv, stickers, postcards, iphones

Through this project, I wanted to bring in the community and collaborative aspect of collage, spinning the prototypical idea of a personal journal into a more ephemeral, interactive, and unexpected piece.

Participants can connect to this digital canvas by scanning a QR code on their phones, which allows them to add and manipulate gifs to create a constantly changing, audio-visual installation.

Digital Canvas

If no users have interacted with the site yet, this is the landing page! It sends users to the QR code link and constantly populates and depopulates the page with random gifs, so that the site lives and changes on its own.

Picking a sprite

The QR codes leads to the page on the left, where participants can select any sprite (out of over 50!) to place on the canvas.

Once a user clicks on a sprite, they're taken to the page on the right. The sprite will automatically be seen on the canvas, and here, the users can manipulate how it looks and sounds. For instance, they can move it vertically and horizontally across the canvas, flip the gif, play its sound, etc.
